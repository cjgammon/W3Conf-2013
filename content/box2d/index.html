Once upon a time..

there was a furry little creature who was struggling to find his way home. 

Use arrow keys to roll, or click and drag to pan around.
Rolling was his way of getting around. He loved to roll around all day. He'd roll through fields and hay.
He was friendly and round and fell to the ground. He'd whistle, sing, and play. He loved to JUMP! And Jump he would he'd jump as far as he possibly could.
It was a sunny day, shiny and bright. There wasn't a storm cloud or frown in sight. The sky was blue, the grass was green.
He rolled up and down and in and out, winding and weaving all about. Through caves and cracks he bobbed and bowled.
